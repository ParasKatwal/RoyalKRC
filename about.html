Royal KRC a vision

RoyalKRC has been dedicated to providing its services to schools all over Nepal. From stationary needs to class material anything necessary for the class functioning is available.

Our Mission


To provide all scholar needs in a single place

Royal KRC founded in the year 2015 A.D. has had dramatic growth periods over the last decade. Facilitating the needs of various schools and universities all over Nepal.

The massive growth in schools and Montessori within the Kathmandu valley leads to the shortage in supplies and materials required. To overcome this hitch Royal KRC Export Import Pvt. Ltd. or Royal KRC in short was initiated.
